Fries Recipe

Description

Learn the secrect to making the most delicious homemade french fries from russet potatoes right at home.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cooking Oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Slice the Potatoes.
  2. Soak them cold in water.
  3. Rinse them twice.
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees.
  5. Increae Heat to 400 degrees.
  6. Place them on paper towerls.
